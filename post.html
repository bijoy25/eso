How to make SEO friendly websites in 2025

Published: Sep 20, 2025 • By Bijoy

Intro

Search engines value helpful content, fast pages and good structure. Follow these steps.

1. Meta Tags & Titles

Use unique titles & meta descriptions per page. Keep title ~50-60 chars and description ~120-160 chars.

2. Mobile & Speed

Make site responsive and optimize images, use caching and CDN for better speed.

3. Schema

Add JSON-LD for organization and article to improve search result appearance.
